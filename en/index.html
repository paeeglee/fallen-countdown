// Chapter 04 — The timeline

From 1.6 to CS2,
never missing a round.

A career that spanned three Counter-Strike versions, a long list of organizations, and the building of an entire scene.

Gabriel "FalleN" Toledo turned pro when Brazil was still a small dot on Counter-Strike's global map. In just over a decade, he turned a scrappy local scene into one of the most respected schools of play in the world.

More than producing individual stars, FalleN built infrastructure: training, in-game leading, the development of young players, and a fanbase that fills arenas. The 2016 Major title wasn't a fluke — it was the payoff of years spent building an entire ecosystem.

At 35, with the CS:GO → CS2 transition behind him, he announced that December 31, 2026 will be his last official match. This page is here to mark the countdown, no rush.
